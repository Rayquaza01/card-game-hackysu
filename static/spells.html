Fireball

This spell deals two extra damage for one turn.

Inspire

This spell increases the damage dealt by the lowest priority, non-spell card by two.

Harden

This spell increases the health of the highest priority, non-spell card by two.

Sun Card

This spell heals the player for two health. Player health cannot exceed 30 HP.

Draw

This spell draws two cards into your hand.

Shield

This spell adds two additional health to the player. Player health cannot exceed 35 HP.

Fearsome Presence

This spell causes the opponent's lowest priority, non-spell card to do zero damage for 1 turn.

Empower

This spell causes a random, non-spell card on the player's active playfield to be replaced with a more powerful variant.
